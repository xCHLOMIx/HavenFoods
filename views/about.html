  About Us


Welcome to HavenFoods, where we revolutionize the way offices and various locations enjoy their meals. Since our inception, we've been dedicated to providing hassle-free meal solutions that cater to diverse dietary needs. With our unique approach, you won't need to worry about ordering specific dishes – instead, you'll enjoy the convenience of our daily meal plans. Whether you opt for our regular meal plan or require special accommodations like vegan options, we've got you covered. Our team ensures that each meal is carefully crafted with quality ingredients, cooked to perfection, and delivered fresh to your location. Say goodbye to mealtime stress and hello to delicious, wholesome lunches with HavenFoods.

Our Goals   

1. Facilitate a sustainable, traceable, and ethical food economy.
2. Support the preservation of ecosystems while enabling small-scale farmers and producers to access the market.
3. Serve as a conduit for transparent communication across the value chain to enable our partners to make informed decisions.
4. Protect our customers’ interests through exemplary service levels and proactive information sharing.
